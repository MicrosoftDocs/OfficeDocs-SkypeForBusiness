# Phone number management for the United Kingdom

Use this table to find information on getting and managing phone numbers in the United Kingdom for Microsoft Teams.
  
For more information, see [Manage phone numbers for your organization](manage-phone-numbers-for-your-organization.md).
  
|**What you want to do?**|**Download the correct forms**|
|:-----|:-----|
|Get new user or service phone numbers that aren't listed in the Microsoft Teams admin center.   <br/> |Download this form. <br/> [New phone number request for the United Kingdom (v.5.0) (en-gb)](https://download.microsoft.com/download/e/e/9/ee9f629e-4f2b-41c3-8fa3-f206660f4db3/new-phone-number-request-for-the-united-kingdom-(v.5.0)-(en-gb).pdf) <br/><br/>Fill it out and [contact the TNS Service Desk for instructions](contact-tns-service-desk.md).  <br/> |
|Get phone numbers transferred to us.  <br/> |Download this form: <br/> [Letter of Authorization (LOA) for the United Kingdom (all numbers) (v.6.1) (en-us)](https://download.microsoft.com/download/4/3/7/43706e72-81e9-461e-afca-825dad4716f4/letter-of-authorization-(loa)-for-the-u.k.-(all-numbers)-(v.6.1)-(en-us).pdf) <br/> <br/> Fill it out and [contact the TNS Service Desk for instructions](contact-tns-service-desk.md).  <br/> |
|Publish/unpublish phone number(s) in the national phone directory.  <br/> |[Contact the TNS Service Desk for instructions](contact-tns-service-desk.md) regarding the list of phone numbers you want to publish/unpublish. <br/> |
|Change a user's number to a service number.  <br/> |[Contact the TNS Service Desk for instructions](contact-tns-service-desk.md) regarding the list of user numbers you want to convert to service numbers. <br/> |
|Make directory service updates to call queue or auto attendant numbers|To add or change call queue or auto attendant numbers, contact [the TNS Service Desk for instructions](contact-tns-service-desk.md). |
